About Us

Neeru menthol Private Limited, Rampur


Established in 2018, SMJ electrical private limited is all in one electrical contract-based company that deals in electrical, mechanical and solar turnkey projects across India. A friendship converted into business partnership in 2016 as “Tesla Engineering Enterprises” that covered multiple projects. As the relationship and our hard work blossomed, all friends together shaped their partnership into “SMJ electrical private limited.”

Our company provides services for Electrical & Mechanical Turnkey Projects, turnkey solar EPC project, solar I&C project, Solar Street Lights projects, operation and maintenance of substations, buildings, pump house, solar plants.

We provide the best association of energy from sunlight to electricity that can have a positive, indirect positive effect on the environment across India. What makes us stands out is that our engineering expertise provides you quick understanding and response to clients’ application needs that includes needs in power, supporting electronics and better performance under varying environmental conditions.
